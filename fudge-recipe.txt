Mama's quickie fudge

1 lb confectioner's sugar
6 heaping TBS cocoa powder
1 stick of butter or margarine
1 TBS vanilla extract
~ 6 TBS milk

Combine all ingredients in heavy saucepan. Stir like crazy
until butter melts and everything is more or less smooth &
chocolaty. Pour into greased 8x8 cake pan and refrigerate.
Cut into squares and enjoy!
